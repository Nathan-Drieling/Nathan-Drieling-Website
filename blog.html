February 1st, 2025: The Artifical Intelligence Race Between the USA and China

New York Times Bestselling author Kai-Fu Lee's book, "AI Superpowers: China, Silicon Valley, And The New World Order" Warned of the eventual AI race between the major hedgemonic powers of the United States and China. Written in 2018, it was far ahead in its predictions for how the world would function and how society would adapt in the eventual rise of advanced intelligent systems. He describes how even chess grandmasters couldn't compete with machines considering every possible move and counter-move available and how few could match an intelligent machines ability to algorithmically predict each move that its opponent would make. And his book was only written in 2018, leaving only to wonder if he had written a sequel could he describe the advances being made.
